I’m a Spanish artisan, designer and architect based in Italy. My work is based on the application of craftsmanship in objects and spaces design with a contemporary approach.
Influenced by the Mediterranean traditions and culture of my hometown – Valencia – my work develops around ceramics and textiles. Each creation is the result of the union between design and craftsmanship and emphasizes the beauty of handmade things and their imperfections

Latest Projects

A selection of projects that are the result of experimentation with natural materials, attention to detail and craftsmanship techniques. Artwork, ceramics and clothing on commission, interior designs and custom furniture.

Services

Workshop & lessons

Monographic courses in group to approach the world of ceramics or sewing and private lessons to deepen or follow a longer training path in one of these disciplines.

Architecture & Interiors

Constructive, functional and aesthetic solutions for the spaces of existing or new architectures. Renovations, furnishing, decoration, coverings and finishes solutions.

Commissioned ceramics

Personalized tableware with initials or writings, plaques for shops, house numbers, corporate gifts, bespoke vases, decorative ceramics or tableware for restaurant sector.

Bespoke artworks

Textile art, ceramic wall sculptures and custom-made ceramic panels. Decorative solutions or adhoc installations for domestic spaces or hospitality.

Made-to-measure clothes

Design and tailor-made clothing for everyday use or for a special occasion. From the fabric to the creation of the shape that best suits the client's body and needs.

Bespoke furniture

Design of custom-made ceramic lighting for domestic spaces or hospitality facilities. Customization of the furniture product according to the interior design.
